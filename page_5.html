Removing makeup and cleansing the skin satisfies her need for regular cleanliness. The fact is that applying makeup or cosmetics without complete pre-cleansing is ineffective: the skin and its pores retain impurities, which contributes to the appearance of pimples and minor imperfections and accelerates its aging. In the morning, the skin also needs to get rid of traces of dust, sweat and night cream residue. For gentle gentle cleansing of the skin without compromising its balance, use gentle formulas and active ingredients suitable for your skin type.
