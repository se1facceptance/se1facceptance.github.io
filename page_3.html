Interestingly, the serum molecules are smaller than the cream molecules, their number is greater. That is why serums have the ability to penetrate deep into the skin, quickly absorbed by cells and solve any skin problems that even a good cream is sometimes powerless to face. It is no coincidence that serum is called a beauty concentrate.With increased pigmentation, freckles and uneven skin tone, a brightening serum is recommended. With excessive dryness and flaking of the skin, a moisturizing serum is required, which normalizes the water balance in the upper layers of the epidermis and eliminates skin tightness.
